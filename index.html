Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: June 2025

Overview

Choice Randomizer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Decision History: Your choices and results are stored locally on your device only.
  • Advertising ID: We use advertising identifiers to show relevant ads (with your permission via App Tracking Transparency).

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Your actual decision content (stays on your device)

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze app performance and crashes

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. Their privacy policy can be found at Google Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All your decision history is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it's never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
