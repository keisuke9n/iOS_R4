Choice Randomizer does not collect any personal information. All your data stays on your device. We don't track you, we don't analyze you, and we don't share your information with anyone because we simply don't have it.

Metamorph ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Choice Randomizer mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information Collection

Choice Randomizer is designed with privacy in mind. The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Choice History: Your previous choices and results
  • App Settings: Pro version status and weight preferences
Important: All data is stored using iOS UserDefaults on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or performance data
  • Any form of user tracking data

3. How We Use Information

Since all data remains on your device, it is used solely to provide the App's functionality:

  • Display your choice history
  • Remember your Pro version status
  • Save weight preferences for choices
  • Enable quick reuse of previous choice sets

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data is stored locally using iOS's built-in UserDefaults system. This means:

  • Data is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Data may be included in your iCloud backups if you have them enabled
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All" button in the History view to delete all saved choices
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all associated data

5. Third-Party Services

In-App Purchases

The Pro version is offered through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple according to their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

Choice Randomizer is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app's privacy-first design ensures it is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be communicated through an app update description.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information for us to share, sell, or disclose. You have complete control over your data as it never leaves your device.
